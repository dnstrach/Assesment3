Dominque Strachan Profile

Travel

I was born and raised in Southern California. Traveling is on the top of my interests because it expands culture and perspective. My favorite kind of climate to travel to is tropical.



Life Outside of Work

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with family and my boyfriend, Rostin. I recently became an aunt which has been truly exciting. I am also a major foodie and LOVE to cook. I've been watching the cooking show for as long as I can remember. Some of my favorite meals to cook are tacos, pasta, and bbq ribs. If I had to choose what my last meal would be from the following, I would choose In-N-Out.

In addition to where I like to spend my quality time, I love to play sports. I come from a family of athletes. My great grandfather was an all American athlete and coached football at Notre Dame University. My dad played basketball at University of Reno and still holds his record. As for me, I played basketball for 8 years and then transitioned to volleyball in high school.
